The purpose of this web-site is to create a digital edition for the course Digital Text in the Humanities’course of the Master Degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge of the University of Bologna, under prof. Tiziana Mancinelli.

The selected test is the story of The Jewels from Guy De Maupassant: a french writer and playwright of the 19th century, famous to be one of the esponents of the modern tale.

This project was born with the idea of finding and inspecting the techniques used by the unreliable narrator, in order to know how he builds and tricks the readers' perceptions.

This is the source of the text https://drsonachs.weebly.com/uploads/5/6/9/2/56928471/the_jewels_full_text.pdf.
